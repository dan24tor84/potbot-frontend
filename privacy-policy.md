# Privacy Policy

## 1. Overview

PotBot is the world’s first AI-powered cannabis scanner designed to analyze buds and plants for quality, mold, and other key factors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data across our app and website.

We value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information.

---

## 2. What We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use PotBot:

- **Photos** of cannabis products you upload
- **Device info** (model, OS version, camera permission status)
- **Location data** (if you use Grower Mode with GPS enabled)
- **Email** (if you sign up for premium features or rank submissions)
- **Crash data** (to improve performance and detect bugs)

We **do not collect**:
- Your personal contacts
- Your ID or government information
- Payment info (handled securely via Google Play)

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data for:
- Generating **Dank Scores**
- Detecting **mold and pests** (using AI inference from open-source models)
- Ranking buds anonymously (for community engagement)
- Providing **growing tips and reminders**
- Improving app performance and accuracy

We **never sell** your data to third parties.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We only share data with:
- **AI processing partners** (like Replicate) to analyze image submissions
- **Firebase** (for authentication, analytics, and crash reporting)
- **MongoDB** (for secure backend data storage)

---

## 5. Cookies and Tracking

On the website ([rankyourdank.com](https://www.rankyourdank.com)), we may use cookies to:
- Remember your dark mode or app view preference
- Track clicks on affiliate links (Amazon)

Cookies are minimal and non-invasive. We do not use ad tracking or invasive analytics.

## 6. Data Security

We implement strong security measures, including:
- HTTPS encryption
- Secure API endpoints
- OAuth and token-based authentication
- Firewall protections on Railway hosting

We strive to meet compliance with **GDPR**, **CCPA**, and **Play Store policies**.
